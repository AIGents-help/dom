2. Authorization

  1. Property Owner grants Operator permission to access the above property, including launch/recovery from a mutually agreed location on the premises, for the sole purpose of conducting the UAS mission described above.
  2. This authorization is limited to the date(s)/window specified and does not grant any ongoing or future access without separate written authorization.
  3. Operator will conduct all flight operations in compliance with FAA Part 107 and any applicable airspace authorization.

3. Acknowledgments

  1. Property Owner acknowledges that UAS operations involve inherent risks and releases Operator and Drone Operation Management from liability for damages arising from the authorized flight, except to the extent caused by Operator's gross negligence or willful misconduct.
  2. Property Owner confirms they have the legal authority to grant this access.
  3. Operator carries commercial liability insurance and will provide proof of coverage upon request.
